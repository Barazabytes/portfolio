About Me

Hello 👋 I'm Said Baraza — a Computer Engineering student at the University of Dodoma with a deep focus on Edge AI/ML Engineering. I build intelligent systems that run directly on microcontrollers, eliminating cloud dependency and enabling real-time inference at the edge.

I specialize in firmware development with ESP-IDF, deploying quantized TensorFlow Lite models on ESP32, and designing end-to-end IoT solutions from hardware to user-facing web interfaces. My goal is to make AI accessible in resource-constrained environments — smart homes, embedded sensors, and autonomous devices.

Beyond building, I share my knowledge through my YouTube channel and am working toward contributing to AI-powered embedded systems that improve daily life.
